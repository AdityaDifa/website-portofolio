About Me

I was born on October 23rd, 2002 in Bantul, Yogyakarta. Since I was a child, I have always been deeply interested in technology, especially smartphones and computers. My curiosity led me to spend a lot of time experimenting with graphic design, video editing, and content creation — often losing track of time and getting scolded for it.


As I grew older, that passion naturally evolved into an interest in programming. I decided to pursue a degree in Informatics and was accepted into Universitas Pembangunan Nasional “Veteran” Yogyakarta. During my studies, I explored various areas of technology, including:


  • Building websites connected to databases
  • Configuring and connecting network routers
  • Developing simple IoT systems for traffic automation
  • Creating AR-based educational games and quizzes
  • Building mobile applications using Flutter
  • Developing a Machine Learning model for image detection, which became the topic of my final thesis
